# APEX — AI Agents & Intelligence Systems

APEX is a portfolio of specialized AI agents and intelligence systems designed to solve real-world workflows across cybersecurity, productivity, finance, knowledge management, and personal optimisation.

Built as a collection of standalone applications connected through a central Mission Control dashboard, the platform explores how AI can transform information into actionable insights, prioritised decisions, and operational workflows.

---

## Why I Built This

As a cybersecurity professional, I wanted to explore practical applications of AI beyond simple chat interfaces.

The goal was to design systems that assist with real operational workflows such as:

* Email triage and prioritisation
* Threat intelligence analysis
* Vulnerability prioritisation
* Financial insights and decision support
* Personal knowledge management
* Technology and cybersecurity news aggregation
* Personal coaching and optimisation

Each project focuses on a specific problem domain while sharing a consistent user experience, architecture, and design philosophy.

---

## Platform Architecture

```text
APEX Mission Control
│
├── InboxIQ
│   └── Email Intelligence & Prioritisation
│
├── ThreatLens AI
│   └── Threat Intelligence & IOC Investigation
│
├── ExposureIQ
│   └── Vulnerability Prioritisation & Exposure Management
│
├── TechBrief
│   └── Technology & Cybersecurity News Briefings
│
├── CapitalLens
│   └── Financial Analysis & Insights
│
├── Forge
│   └── Fitness Coaching & Personal Optimisation
│
└── Cortex
    └── Persistent Memory & Knowledge Management
```

Apex serves as the orchestration layer, while each system operates independently and focuses on a specific workflow or decision-support function.

## Security & Privacy

This repository is intended for portfolio and demonstration purposes.

Where personal or organisational data would normally be required, simulated datasets are used instead.

Examples include:

* Demo inbox data within InboxIQ
* Demo financial information within CapitalLens
* Simulated enterprise asset inventory within ExposureIQ

ExposureIQ combines live vulnerability intelligence from public sources with simulated enterprise asset context. No organisational systems, infrastructure, or internal data are connected to the public platform.

No personal or organisational data is stored within this public repository.
