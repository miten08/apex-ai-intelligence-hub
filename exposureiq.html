Vulnerability Prioritisation Agent

Combines live vulnerability intelligence from NVD and CISA KEV with organisational asset context to identify which exploited vulnerabilities actually matter most. ExposureIQ maps threat intelligence to asset exposure and business criticality, transforming CVE data into prioritised remediation actions.

Agent purpose
ThreatLens investigates indicators and threat actors. ExposureIQ prioritises remediation by mapping live CVEs to asset exposure, business criticality and patch urgency.
